# Prefa News

Prefa News is a digital platform designed to aggregate, analyze, and amplify positive news and public sector initiatives from municipalities across Santa Catarina, Brazil.

## Overview

Every day, valuable and impactful initiatives are developed by local governments. However, these stories often remain confined to their city of origin, limiting their potential impact.

Prefa News was created to address two fundamental challenges:

- How can successful local initiatives reach citizens more effectively?
- How can municipalities learn from each other and replicate proven solutions?

The platform acts as a bridge between municipal news portals, cataloging and enhancing access to public sector news and projects. It enables:

- Citizens to explore relevant news from their city and region
- Public managers to identify and replicate successful initiatives from other municipalities

## Key Features

- **News Aggregation**: Centralized collection of news from multiple municipal sources
- **Content Categorization**: Structured organization of initiatives and public policies
- **AI-Based Ranking**: Intelligent evaluation and prioritization of content relevance
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Facilitates benchmarking and replication of successful public policies
- **Regional Visibility**: Amplifies high-impact initiatives beyond local boundaries

## How It Works

Prefa News collects and processes news from municipal portals. Each piece of content is analyzed using artificial intelligence, which assigns a relevance score. Based on this score, the platform dynamically positions content to maximize visibility and impact.

This approach ensures that the most relevant and innovative initiatives receive appropriate attention, both from citizens and public administrators.

## Target Audience

- Citizens interested in local and regional public initiatives
- Municipal managers and public administrators
- Policy makers and government analysts
- Researchers and institutions focused on public innovation

## Project Goals

- Increase transparency and accessibility of municipal actions
- Promote the replication of successful public policies
- Strengthen collaboration between municipalities
- Enhance citizen engagement with local governance
